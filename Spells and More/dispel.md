The alias works as if it were to cast dispel magic, except it rolls the check at the same time.

**Valid Arguments (General)**
`-t <target>` - Targets another combatant in combat to make them cast dispel magic if they have the spell slots for it. 
`-abj` - Makes the check with the level 10 Abjuration Wizard Bonus. You also get it automatically as a Level 10 Abjuration Wizard if you set your level with `!level`

**Valid Arguments (Check)**

`adv/dis` - Give advantage/disadvantage to the check roll(s).
`-b <bonus>` - Adds a bonus to the roll.
`-dc <dc>` - Sets a DC and checks if success or failure.
`-mc <minimum roll>` - Sets the minimum roll on the dice.
`-rr` <iterations> - How many checks to roll.

**Valid Arguments (Casting)**
`-i` - Ignores Spellbook restrictions (e.g. when casting from an item, as a ritual, etc.)
`-l <#>` - Specifies the level to cast the spell at.
