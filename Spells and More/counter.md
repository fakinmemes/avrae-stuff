The alias works as if it were to cast counterspell, except it rolls the check at the same time, and if !level or the argument -abj is used, can also take into account the Abjuration Wizard's bonus to roll.

**Valid Arguments (Check)**
`adv/dis` - Give advantage/disadvantage to the check roll(s).
`-b bonus` - Adds a bonus to the roll.
`-dc dc` - Sets a DC and checks if success or failure.
`-mc <minimum roll>` - Sets the minimum roll on the dice.

**Valid Arguments (Casting)**
`-i` - Ignores Spellbook restrictions (e.g. when casting from an item, as a ritual, etc.)
`-l # `- Specifies the level to cast the spell at.
